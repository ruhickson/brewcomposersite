Market Insights

Current Market State

With 9,796 craft breweries operating in the US alone, the market represents a significant opportunity for BrewComposer. Craft breweries are defined as producing no more than 6 million barrels per year (7,040,865 HL).

Production Volume

Craft breweries produced a combined 23.1 million barrels in 2024. When spread evenly across all breweries, this shows an average of 2,348 barrels per brewery, highlighting the diverse range of operations in the market.

Market Opportunity

While there may be a slight downward trend in the market, this actually highlights the importance of what BrewComposer brings: clear line of sight and stability. In challenging market conditions, breweries need tools that provide operational efficiency and cost savings more than ever.

Proven Demand

Our consulting experience across 50+ customers demonstrates strong market demand for brewing optimization solutions. From small craft breweries to large contract manufacturing facilities, the need for better process control and cost management is universal.

Target Market Segments

Home Brewers

Individuals or hobbyists producing up to 1 barrel annually. Ideal for learning, experimentation, and small-batch production. Perfect candidates for a self-service subscription solution.

Microbreweries

Breweries producing 10-100 barrels annually, often focused on local markets and specialty products. Perfect candidates for a self-service subscription solution.

Small Craft Breweries

Breweries producing 100-1,000 barrels annually, seeking to optimize operations and reduce costs. Perfect candidates for a self-service subscription solution.

Mid-Size Breweries

Breweries producing 1,000-15,000 barrels annually, looking to scale efficiently and maintain quality.

Large Craft Breweries

Breweries producing 15,000-6,000,000 barrels annually, needing enterprise-level process control.

Contract Manufacturing

Facilities like Big Kettle Brewing Company that serve multiple customers and need robust quality control systems.
